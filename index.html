ABOUT ME

I live in San Francisco and am driving an exciting redirect of my engineering life from Computational Fluid Dynamics into web development. Programming was one of the most enjoyable parts of my engineering career: writing custom functions to extend commercial simulation software, data analysis in Matlab, Scheme scripts for automatizing post processing visualization of a fluid flow simulation, and I can even recall a few FORTRAN scripts in Engineering classes.

What was missing in all this fun was a personal connection to the systems I was analyzing. Enter JavaScript and web development. I realized that I could take my programming experience and connect it to something visual and personal.

I love the logic of software development and the challenge of finding the best tools and algorithms to build amazing things.

ABOUT ME

I live in San Francisco and am a software engineer, interested in full stack web development. I recently worked on a mobile app to be released in the app store Q2 2014 and a data visualization project using d3.js to show a visual node-cloud relationship between wikipeidia articles. Over the past year I have transitioned from an engineering career in Materials Science and Computational Fluid Dynamics. Programming was one of the most enjoyable parts of my engineering career: writing custom functions to extend commercial simulation software, data analysis in Matlab, Scheme scripts for automatizing post processing visualization of a fluid flow simulation, and I can even recall a few FORTRAN scripts in Engineering classes.

What was missing in all this fun was a personal connection to the systems I was analyzing. Enter JavaScript and web development. I realized that I could take my programming experience and connect it to something visual and personal.

I love the logic of software development and the challenge of finding the best tools and algorithms to build amazing things.
